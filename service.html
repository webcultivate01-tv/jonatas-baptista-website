Three Ways To Work Together

Whichever stage your business is at, the goal stays the same: identify and remove performance leaks, and build the systems that create more predictable results — without relying on constant hustle.

Performance Coaching Business Performance Audit Growth Strategy Sessions
01

Performance Coaching

Ongoing, one-to-one coaching for agents who want sustained improvement in lead generation, client conversion, follow-up and pipeline performance — built on consistent, structured support rather than one-off advice.

Start Performance Coaching

Benefits

  • ●More consistent monthly income, instead of unpredictable peaks and gaps
  • ●Fewer promising opportunities lost to weak or inconsistent follow-up
  • ●Clearer daily and weekly priorities, instead of reacting to whatever feels urgent
  • ●Sharper communication with clients at every stage of the journey
  • ●Decisions guided by what's actually happening in your business, not guesswork

Process

  1. 1.An initial conversation to understand your business, goals and current results
  2. 2.Identifying exactly where performance leaks are occurring across lead generation, conversion, follow-up and pipeline
  3. 3.Building a tailored action plan around your specific business, not a generic script
  4. 4.Ongoing coaching sessions to implement, track and adjust as your results change

Expected Outcomes

  • ●More predictable monthly results
  • ●Stronger handling of buyer hesitation and negotiation dynamics
  • ●A pipeline that reflects reality, organised and easy to act on
  • ●Increased confidence in day-to-day decision-making
02

Business Performance Audit

A focused, outside review of your real estate business across the areas that matter most — lead generation, client conversion, follow-up effectiveness, pipeline management and communication — to identify exactly where income and opportunities are being lost.

Request A Business Audit

Benefits

  • ●Clarity on what's actually happening in your business right now, not assumptions
  • ●A clear, specific list of where performance is leaking
  • ●A prioritised view of what to address first for the greatest impact
  • ●An outside perspective shaped by 20 years across real estate and leadership

Process

  1. 1.You share how your business currently runs — lead sources, conversion steps, follow-up habits, pipeline tracking
  2. 2.Your business is reviewed against the patterns that separate consistently high-performing agents from those who stay stuck
  3. 3.You receive a clear, plain-language breakdown of where opportunities and income are leaking
  4. 4.Findings are discussed together, with next steps outlined

Expected Outcomes

  • ●A precise understanding of where your business is underperforming
  • ●A solid foundation for self-directed improvement or ongoing coaching
  • ●More confidence deciding where to invest your time and effort next
03

Growth Strategy Sessions

Strategic sessions for agents who already have a working business and want to grow it deliberately — stronger systems, sharper communication, and more predictable scaling, rather than chasing every new trend.

Book A Growth Strategy Session

Benefits

  • ●A growth plan grounded in your current numbers, not generic targets
  • ●Stronger systems that scale without constantly being rebuilt
  • ●Sharper communication and storytelling, drawn from six years directing television
  • ●A longer-term view of your business, instead of reacting deal to deal

Process

  1. 1.Reviewing your current performance and growth goals together
  2. 2.Mapping where systemic limits, not effort limits, are holding growth back
  3. 3.Building a structured plan across lead generation, communication and business growth
  4. 4.Scheduling periodic check-ins to track progress and adjust strategy as needed

Expected Outcomes

  • ●A documented growth strategy specific to your business
  • ●Stronger, more repeatable systems behind your day-to-day work
  • ●Increased capacity without proportionally increasing hours worked
  • ●Greater control over the direction your business is heading
