My Story

From Closing Deals To
Understanding Why Deals Close

Jan 2004 — ERA Portugal

The Beginning: €250,000 in Year One

Jonatas began his real estate career in January 2004 as a Real Estate Consultant at ERA Portugal in Lisbon. In his first year alone, he generated €250,000 in commissions for the agency — which was recognised as the best agency in income that year. It was an early, concrete lesson: results in real estate are never random.

2005 — 2018 · Manager & Director

A Decade Watching Why Teams Succeed

From 2005 to 2015 as Real Estate Manager at Palm Real Estate, then as Director at Planet Immo Real Estate (2016–2018), he wasn't just selling — he was watching closely why some agents thrived while others, doing what looked like the same work, did not.

2019 — 2025 · HC TV

Six Years Studying Human Behaviour

Between 2019 and 2025, Jonatas stepped outside real estate entirely, spending six years as a Television Director focused on communication and storytelling. That experience — studying how stories are told, how attention is held, and how people decide — turned out to be directly relevant to everything he had already observed in real estate.

Jan 2026 — Present

Today: Coaching Agents To Close The Gap

Since January 2026, Jonatas has worked as an Independent Strategic Advisor and Real Estate Coach, based in Lisbon. His focus today is on the same question that has run through his entire career: what separates agents who remain stuck from those who continue to grow — and how to close that gap deliberately, without relying on hustle or the latest trend.

Coaching Philosophy

No Fluff. Just Fewer Leaks,
And More Control.

Jonatas's goal as a coach is simple: help agents identify and remove performance leaks, and build the systems, processes and decision-making habits that create more predictable results — more income, more consistency, more control over your business.

01

More Income

Predictable monthly results replace unpredictable peaks and dry spells. You build a business that generates income consistently — not just in good months.

Outcome → Consistent Revenue
02

More Consistency

Systems and habits that sustain performance over time, regardless of market conditions. No more relying on momentum, luck, or a single good lead.

Outcome → Repeatable Systems
03

More Control

Making decisions from clarity, not reacting to what feels urgent each day. You lead your business with intention — instead of being led by it.

Outcome → Confident Decisions
