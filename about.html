About Jonatas Baptista

Twenty Years In Real Estate. One Question That Never Stopped Mattering.

Why do some agents consistently reach €10,000+ months, while others — in the same market, under the same conditions — stay stuck at €2,000–€5,000? That question has shaped Jonatas Baptista's entire career, from real estate consultant to real estate director to television director to, today, real estate coach.

My Story

From Closing Deals To Understanding Why Deals Close

Jonatas began his real estate career in January 2004 as a Real Estate Consultant at ERA Portugal in Lisbon. In his first year alone, he generated €250,000 in commissions for the agency — which was recognised as the best agency in income that year. It was an early, concrete lesson: results in real estate are never random.

From there, he spent a decade, from 2005 to 2015, as Real Estate Manager at Palm Real Estate in Lisbon, followed by two years, from 2016 to 2018, as Director of Real Estate at Planet Immo Real Estate. Across both roles, he wasn't just selling — he was watching closely why some agents on his teams thrived while others, doing what looked like the same work, did not.

Between 2019 and 2025, Jonatas stepped outside real estate entirely, spending six years as a Television Director focused on communication and storytelling. That experience — studying how stories are told, how attention is held, and how people actually respond and decide — turned out to be directly relevant to everything he had already observed in real estate.

Since January 2026, Jonatas has worked as an Independent Strategic Advisor and Real Estate Coach, based in Lisbon. His focus today is on the same question that has run through his entire career: what separates agents who remain stuck from those who continue to grow — and how to close that gap deliberately, without relying on hustle or the latest trend.

Coaching Philosophy

No Fluff. Just Fewer Leaks, And More Control.

Jonatas's goal as a coach is simple: help agents identify and remove performance leaks, and build the systems, processes and decision-making habits that create more predictable results. No fluff. More income. More consistency. More control over your business. More time focused on what matters most.

Those outcomes rarely come from working harder alone. They come from improving performance where it has the greatest impact — and that's exactly where his coaching is focused.

Why Some Agents Succeed

It's Rarely One Tactic. It's How They Operate.

A particular focus of Jonatas's work is understanding why opportunities that initially show promise often fail to progress — by studying the behavioural, communication and organisational factors that influence outcomes throughout the client journey.
