Where it started

Where It Began

Growing Up With
Big Dreams & Empty Pockets.

I grew up in a household where money was tight and expectations were low — at least from the outside. My parents worked hard, loved unconditionally, but never had the luxury of thinking beyond survival.

At 19, I made a decision that changed everything: I would become the person my family didn't even know was possible. Not out of ego — out of deep love and a belief that more was available to all of us.

I read every book I could find. I found mentors who showed me the map. I made mistakes that cost money I didn't have. And through every stumble, I found the principles that actually work.

The Path

Key Moments That Shaped Me

2012

First Steps into Self-Development

Read my first personal development book at 19. Couldn't put it down. Spent every spare dollar on books, seminars, and mentors from that point forward.

2015

First Business Launch — and First Failure

Launched my first venture. It failed within 14 months. Lost nearly everything. But I gained lessons worth more than any MBA — and the resilience to try again.

2017

Discovered My Calling in Coaching

A friend asked me to help him through a career crisis. Three sessions in, he landed his dream role at double the salary. I realised I had a gift for helping people see what they couldn't see themselves.

2020

Scaled to 100+ Clients

Despite (or perhaps because of) the world falling apart, demand for transformation soared. I built systems, brought in frameworks, and crossed 100 active clients for the first time.

TODAY

500+ Lives Changed & Counting

Today I work with executives, entrepreneurs, and professionals across four continents. The mission hasn't changed — help people become who they were always capable of being.
