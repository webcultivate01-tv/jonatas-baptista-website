The Question Behind The Coaching

Why Do Some Agents Consistently Outperform Others?

Every year, in the same market, under the same conditions, some agents struggle to consistently reach €2,000–€5,000 months — while others generate €10,000+ months. Same industry. Same opportunities. Different outcomes. That contrast has fascinated Jonatas throughout his career.

After 20 years of experience as a Real Estate Agent and Real Estate Director, he has spent years observing what separates agents who remain stuck from those who continue to grow. What he has found is that success is rarely determined by a single tactic, script or lead source — the highest-performing agents simply operate differently across several key areas of their business.

"My goal is simple — help agents identify and remove performance leaks, and build the systems that create predictable results."
